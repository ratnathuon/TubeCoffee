Welcome to Tube Coffee!

At Tube Coffee, we are passionate about providing our customers with the best coffee experience possible. Our story began in 2017 when three Cambodian coffee-loving youths came together with a dream to establish a coffee brand that truly represents the Khmer people. With a focus on fast service, affordable prices, and high-quality products, we have grown into the leading coffee and food chain in Cambodia, with 43 stores across the country.

About Us

The story began with 3 Cambodian coffee-lover youths, with coffee brand establishment dream that truly represents Khmer people materializing in 2017 as the first store — a simple but attractive Tube Coffee Kdan Two — being inaugurated. With exceptional emphasis on fast service, affordable price tag, and high-quality products, TUBE COFFEE and TUBE COFFEE + have experienced radical transformation to the leading Khmer people favorite coffee and food chain in Cambodia, indicated by the ever-growing expansion clocking at 43 stores, 8 of which are TUBE COFFEE +.
